When facing an arrest, knowing your rights and the proper steps to take can make a significant difference in your case...

## Know Your Rights

1. Right to remain silent
2. Right to an attorney
3. Right to refuse searches

## Immediate Steps to Take

1. Stay calm and cooperative
2. Don't resist arrest
3. Contact an attorney immediately
